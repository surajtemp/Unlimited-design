    Frequently Asked Questions

    • What does unlimited really mean?

      You can send us as many design requests as you like, we will process them one by one in a queue for you as fast as humanly possible. If you are not happy with any of the design concepts, you can then request as many changes to that concept as you like. You will never be charged more than the monthly fee and there are no other fees that you’ll need to pay to access our design service.

    • Can you adhere to brand & style guides?

      Yes we can. As long as the brand / style guide is provided along with any specific requirements on colours, fonts and sizing, we’ll be able to produce design work within your guidelines.

    • How does your 7-day risk-free money back guarantee work?

      If at any point within your first 14 days from the date of signup you are dissatisfied or feel that this model is not meant for you and if you haven’t approved a design, then you will get a full refund; no questions asked.

    • How long does it take to complete a design request?

      This depends on the design request sent to us and pricing plan you opt for. Our ₹14,000/- unlimited monthly plan offers a 24 hour turnaround time. With ₹8,000/- and ₹3000/- plans, the maximum turnaround time is 48 hours.

    • What if I need something done immediately?

      Haven’t we all been there, where something was needed yesterday. However, to ensure quality of work, we require at least 1 business day to complete a task. You can put in a request to have the design completed urgently which we will try our best to adhere to but if your requirement is an immediate turn around (e.g less than 8 hours), it may be best to reach out to a local freelancer to see whether they can provide something quicker.

    • Still have questions?

      Email us at hello@graphicdesign.io.com or hit the chat button on your right to chat with us right away.
